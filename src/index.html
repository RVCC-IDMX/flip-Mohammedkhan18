Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Pumpkin Pie is one of the most iconic fall desserts. This recipe is simple yet perfect in taste and texture!!

  • 1 9-inch Homemade pie crust , or store-bought, deep dish, unbaked
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 15 ounce can canned pumpkin or fresh pumpkin puree
  • 12 ounce can evaporated milk
